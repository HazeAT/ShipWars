Chapter I

The Adventure Begins

Welcome to an expansive universe where you can become the master of the stars!
In this WPF game, you can buy and sell starships in bustling markets, both local and online.
Whether you choose a life of legality or dive into the shadows with illicit jobs,
your destiny is in your hands.

Travel to legendary planets from the Star Wars saga, each filled with opportunities and dangers.
Own an impressive fleet of ships, and when the time comes, sell them back or
put them up for grabs in the online market where players from across the galaxy can buy them.

Embark on daring missions to steal valuable goods from distant worlds and sell them for profit,
or take on jobs that promise riches and adventure. Renting ships is also an option
for those who need a temporary vessel for their exploits.

The galaxy is vast, and your journey has just begun. Gather your crew, set your course,
and make your mark in the stars...
